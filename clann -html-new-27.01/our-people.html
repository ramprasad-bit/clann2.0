Our People

What We Stand For

We are passionate about results that matter and are committed to constantly challenging the way we do things, so that we can be the best at what we do. We aim to set the benchmark for housing quality and customer service. We accept only the highest standards of conduct and put service before self-interest.

Steve Loveland

Head of Clann

Steve has over 20 years of experience in Housing, working across supported accommodation services, homelessness services and social housing. Previously responsible for the coordination and delivery of Clúid’s Resident Engagement Strategy, Steve inspired continued improvement and uptake of resident engagement initiatives that promote participation while widening structures that enable residents to feedback and influence how services are delivered and developed.

Maureen Batley

Scheme Manager

Maureen has over 12 years experience providing general and sheltered housing services. She works with a highly dedicated team to bring an excellent range of services to residents and friends of St Joseph’s Court. Her ethos is the long term sustainability of older residents in age friendly housing within their own community where they have access to family, friends, services and support.

Rachel Lartey

Housing Manager

With over 20 years’ experience in social housing, neighbourhood services and regeneration, Rachel leads our team of Housing Coordinators and Scheme Managers. She is committed to the continual improvement of Clann’s housing service, driving projects which promote equality, inclusion, value for money, and quality.

Davina Mooney

Housing Coordinator

Davina joined Clann in 2016 having previously worked in Media and Politics. She has experience in managing both general and age-friendly housing schemes. Davina holds a BA in Communications from Mary Immaculate College and a Postgraduate Diploma in Public Relations from the Institute of Public Relations. During her time with Clann, she has attained further qualifications in Housing, Advocacy, Change Leadership, Understanding Conflict, Mediation Theory and Practice.

Deirdre O’Donnell

Housing Coordinator

With over 10 years’ experience in age-friendly housing provision, Deirdre coordinates a team of scheme managers, night wardens and caretakers. She has direct experience working with older people and is committed to ensuring our residents can remain living independently for as long as they wish. Deirdre’s role covers schemes in Cavan, Donegal, Dublin, Galway, and Louth.
